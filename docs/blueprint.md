# **App Name**: Figurinha Craque

## Core Features:

- Progressive Funnel Quiz: A 4-step gamified journey on a vibrant yellow background featuring a blue progress bar and pagination dots to track user advancement.
- Smart Photo Upload & Preview: Integration with gallery and camera for children's portraits, including a circular validation preview to ensure the face is properly framed.
- AI Sticker Generation tool: A tool that uses reasoning to stylize portraits into high-quality soccer card art while strictly maintaining the child's likeness and applying dynamic text data.
- Firebase Lead & Storage Management: Secure storage of lead data (name, age, stats) in Firestore and private image hosting in Firebase Storage with restricted access.
- Dynamic Watermarked Preview: Display a pre-payment preview of the collectible card with a repeated 'PREVIA' watermark and custom stats (Weight, Height, Club).
- Loading & Upsell Experience: An animated loading screen with a simulated percentage bar and a vibrant green prize highlight to increase conversion during AI processing.
- Checkout & Fulfillment: Integration with payment flows to unlock the high-resolution, watermark-free digital file after confirmation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dominant Background: Strong vibrant yellow (#FFD400) filling 70% of the screen across all screens for a Brazilian tournament aesthetic.
- Action Elements: Deep Royal Blue (#183A9E) for primary buttons, progress bar fills, active pagination dots, and main titles.
- Surfaces: Pure White (#FFFFFF) cards with 24px rounded corners and soft shadows to house all quiz inputs and content.
- Success Accents: Vibrant Green (#00A84F) used exclusively for prize highlights (e.g., 'MIL REAIS') and the final price tag.
- Sporty Headlines: Thick, bold poster-style fonts in deep blue to provide high contrast and an energetic soccer-themed feel.
- Secondary Text: Clean sans-serif fonts in soft black or grey for instructions, placeholders, and supporting labels.
- Mobile-First Verticality: Centered vertical layout with generous spacing, designed to fit perfectly on mobile screens without horizontal scrolling.
- Gamified Emojis: Use of standard playful soccer and utility emojis (✍️, 🎂, ⭐, ⚽, ⚠️) centered at the top of each white card.
- Interactive Transitions: Smooth sliding transitions between quiz steps and a pulse effect on the primary CTA buttons.
